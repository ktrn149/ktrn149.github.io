New Start, New Career

So why computer science? Several reasons...firstly, it's going to be in demand for a while. Everyone and their kid brother has a computer, and professionals are needed to either create websites, software, or applications that people can use on a multitude of devices. In addition, it's a career in which one can potentially enter without a formal education, and have many career and work location options. Even though I've used computers since I was a kid, this is the first time I've taken seriously as a profession. I know that it's going to be a long and challenging journey, but I'm excited to learn and slowly make progress.

Korea: Going Back to my Second Home

I have a strong affection for the country and people of Korea. After living there for three years, I've become a big fan of the country. The history, culture, cuisine, langauge of Korea has been a never-ending source of interest for me. To this day, there's a lot I still haven't done or seen in South Korea.

My goals for living in Korea:

1. Contiune to learn about and experence Korea, and educate students in learning English

2. Save a sizeable portion of my income to put towards future endevors in computer science

3. Use the next 2-3 years as a launching platform to enter the tech industry. I recognize that it will take time to study and create projects, so I want to use my time abroad effectively
